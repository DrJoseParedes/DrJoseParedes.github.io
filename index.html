José A. Paredes — Research

Dr José Antonio Paredes Moreno is a Lecturer at the University of Roehampton. He develops advanced sensing systems for smart environments, health monitoring, and privacy‑aware technologies, with a focus on millimetre‑wave radar, infrared time‑of‑flight cameras, and AI.
