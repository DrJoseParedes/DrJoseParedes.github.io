About

Hello! I am Jose Paredes

I am a lecturer at the University of Roehampton, London, UK. My research focuses on the application of millimetrewave radar technology for environmental analysis. This includes tracking individuals and developing navigation systems for drones. I have a strong background in signal processing, machine learning, and embedded systems. I am passionate about teaching and mentoring students in engineering and computer science.
